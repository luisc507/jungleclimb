The outdoors climbing in Panamá is mostly bouldering and sport climbing during the dry season. Conglomerate and basalt Rock are characteristic of the country. The sectors are still under development with around 30 to 50 routes each.

Most of the spots near Panamá are anywhere from 1 hour to 3 hours from the city or in Chiriquí (Boquete mostly). All of them on private land so fallow the access rules.

From the beginning of january to mid april during summer. The climbing conditions in Panamá are humid and hot all year round however still climbable.
